This Site

This site facilitates learning and vApp deployment for Dakota State University's DefSec club. C.O.D.Y. was written to be as fast and to-the-point as possible. It is open source.

Learning

The Learn tab contains interactive, hands-on micro-courses (μCourses?) put together by DSU's Defensive Security Club.

Deploying

The Deploy tab will deploy a specified vApp to your account. It is a simple alternatve to the IALab's interface.
